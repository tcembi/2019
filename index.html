Vox Chamber Choir Logo

Vox, a choir with a unique sound, were looking for a logo which would be representative of their sound. Vox’s music has an enchanting sound as they replicate sounds from nature and infuse it into their music. They view their music as a spiritual journey and I used that as a base for my logo design. My aim with the logo that I have designed was to evoke a feeling of flow in a symbol that would feel at home on a musical score sheet

I made three logos that could work for the brand. Each logo had a unique story and each could be incorporated into the look and feel of the brand.


Deca Music Video

Deca is an instrumental band that wanted to conceptualise and shoot a music video for their cover of Ed Sheeran’s "Shape of you" song. The music video was shot for Deca’s Youtube channel to generate views and increase their followers.
This was a great experience for me, as this was my first time editing a music video intended for professional use.

Grumpy App

This is a design for an app called Grumpy. The brief was to design the CI for Grumpy. Dedicated to venting out complaints, this app allows people to voice out their complaints and vent out their anger on a social media platform.

After many iterations, the client was fixated on the “G” character for the logo. The logo was to represent communication and anger yet keep a clean corporate style that could translate well for an app icon. The UI design is inspired by simplicity as no one would want to pester an already angry user.

I designed emojis that had angry expressions and they were added onto the app, as a way for users to express themselves.

Champion Ad

This is a stop motion ad of which we were required to select a brand of our choice to promote through creating a stop motion ad. This ad promotes the sweet Champion.

The sweet is made into a character who has an athletic obstacle to go through and ends with it parachuting like a hero with other sweets aspiring to be like the Champion. The ad ends with a phrase of “A Champion will always be a Champion”.

Watermelon

The following illustration is a metaphor of who I am, how I view myself, and my likes. I am a big fan of fresh fruit and sweet things. I also have an interest in innovation, which is my reason for choosing a cube shaped watermelon. The cubed watermelon is a Chinese innovation, which they invented for easy packing and transportation. The cubed watermelon reflects my love for finding new ways of doing things. I animated the cubed watermelon. So, it eats itself until only the hands and mouth are left. This is a reflection of how hard I am on myself when I work, to a point where it often consumes me. The animation ends with my name typed in a font-face I had created to suit the look and feel of the watermelon.

My first love is Illustrator, therefore, I started my character development using Illustrator. My experience on animating characters on After Effects was limited, at the time, so I enlisted the help of one of the designers that I worked with. He showed me the basics and I quickly learnt how to animate by myself.

Open Data Cause

Open Data logo

The Open data logo is done in an 8bit style. This gives the feeling of a digital format as the whole campaign is based on making people aware of cheaper alternatives to make internet access cheaper. Kore Dore is the slogan/chatch phrase for the social media campaign. This is short for Know more, do more.

This project is about thinking of a Cause that could help humanity whilst aligning it with a brand. The aim of this cause is to enlighten people about affordable ways to access the internet. We were expected to come up with the logo and overall brand for the which ever cause we chose.
